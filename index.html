Privacy Policy — Sharp Jokes

Application name (as in HUAWEI AppGallery Connect): Sharp Jokes

Developer / Publisher (as in HUAWEI AppGallery Connect): Siarhei Piachko

Last updated: May 2026 (app update 1.0.0). Sharp Jokes is a jokes app in a card-style UI. Privacy questions: regius.lab@gmail.com.

1. Data we collect (on your device)

This app stores data locally on your device using local storage (for example SharedPreferences or similar on-device storage). The app does not require account registration. We do not operate our own backend servers to collect or store your joke content or app settings.

Depending on how you use the app, local data may include:

  • Favorites — jokes you mark as favorites;
  • App preferences — such as theme (system/light/dark), joke language (English or Russian), enabled joke categories, reader font size, and similar settings;
  • Deck position — where you left off in the joke feed (per language), so the app can restore your place;
  • Disclaimer acceptance — whether you have acknowledged the first-launch notice.

Built-in catalog: Jokes included with the app are stored in the application package and are available offline. Reading them does not send the joke text to us.

My Jokes (personal notebook): If you use the “My Jokes” feature, the joke texts you create, edit, or delete are stored only on your device, in separate notebooks per joke language (English / Russian). We do not upload your own jokes to our servers. You can remove them by deleting individual jokes in the app or by clearing the app’s data / uninstalling the app.

Personalized recommendations: When you use the recommendation feature, suggestions are calculated on your device based on jokes you saved as favorites (and, where applicable, recent jokes shown in the current session). We do not upload your favorites or joke history to our own servers for this feature.

We do not ask for your contacts, precise location, photos, microphone access, or other sensitive device data for the app’s own features.
